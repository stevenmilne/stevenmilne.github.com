The Northern Lights Conference is now in its 4th year, and will take place on Friday, 17 October in the auditorium of historic King’s College, Aberdeen. Tickets are just £15, including lunch and refreshments.

A bargain for a much needed energy boost as the nights draw in and winter approaches.

Northern Lights is always a little different, this year is no exception. We have an exciting programme of speakers, open space sessions, and a couple of new surprises planned. We're moving to the bigger auditorium this year, and with the free CodeTheCity civic hacking weekend kicking off the following day it's a great time for geeks to be in the city.
