About

The Northern Lights conference was started in 2011 with a simple aim:

to provide a one-day enthusiasm booster shot for geeks of all creeds that inspires us to better ourselves and our communities.

As we plan the 2013 conference we are continuing with this goal.

Past events:

2012

In 2012 the theme for the day was community, and a great roster of speakers tackled this from various angles. Keynotes from Katrina Owen and Paul Campbell, panel discussions and talks from Adrian Mowat, Gillian Easson, Giovanni Intini, Hilary Singer Roberts, Phil Roberts, Rich Quick, and Ryan Shuttleworth

You can see much more from the day on the original website here.

2011

The first Northern Lights conference had two great keynote speakers. Joe O'Brien, then of EdgeCase, and Tom Preston-Warner, cofounder of Github. The closing keynote that Tom gave on the blackboard was inspiring both for the story itself, and his excellent chalk skills.
